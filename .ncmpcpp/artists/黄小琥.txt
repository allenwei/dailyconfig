Tiger Wong is a veteran Taiwanese Chinese and English pop artist who is also known as "The Queen of Pub" for her extensive experience performing live in pubs and restaurants. She was born in 1963 in Kaohsiung, Taiwan. After releasing her first studio album in 1990, she has since become a well-respected figure in the region's music industry. In 2007 she was invited to become a judge in Taiwan's hit singing competition One Million Star, where she gave the participants some strict and sharp remarks. Tiger also performed in the opening and closing ceremonies of The World Games 2009 Kaohsiung.
